MCPC First Newsletter of the Year
Hi everyone! We hope you've been enjoying your semester so far. We're nearing the end of the semester and have got a lot planned before we head off into SWOTVAC and exams.
We've got some very exciting events and collaborations coming up soon that we can't wait to share with you. We are also super excited to introduce to you our new committee members!
We also have a new linktree so that you can access all of our social media on one page. Make sure to follow us on all socials to keep up to date with events and more!
📰 Breaking News 📰
🎟️ Discounted Supanova Tickets 🎟️
Supanova is coming to Melbourne on the 22nd and 23rd of May, and we have collaborated with the Fantasy And Sci-Fi Association (FASA) to bring you DISCOUNTED Supanova Tickets!
Both FASA and Cosplaty will be selling a limited amount of SUNDAY (23rd of May) tickets with prices depending on these conditions:

MSA Rewards Subscriber ONLY: $28.48
Clayton Student ONLY: $28.48
MSA Rewards Subscriber AND Clayton Student: $19.10

These prices are based on the full ticket price (RRP) of $37.50!
Please note that you must be a member of Monash Cosplay Club to get these tickets.
The forms also close on THURSDAY THE 20th AT 11:59PM.
Click here to access the expression of interest form.
Once you fill out the form, we will send you an invoice. Please pay this and send a screenshot to confirm your payment in reply to your invoice.
Once this is done, we will purchase tickets for everyone who got in on time to the forms, and will keep you updated on how the ticket will be given to you.
These tickets are first in, best dressed, so get in fast and hope to see you there!
📅 Upcoming Events 📅
🥪 Supanova After Picnic 🥪
If you're heading to Supanova on Sunday the 23rd of May, Fantasy And Sci-Fi Association (FASA) and us have planned a Supanova After Picnic so that members can meet up together!
Come relax and unwind for our post-Supanova celebration with FASA at 6:00pm on the 23rd of May at the Melbourne Showgrounds. Make sure to bring some food, and feel free to bring something to share between everyone if you're feeling generous 😋.
Keep updated on the details of the Supanova After Picnic through our Facebook event!
☕ Cosplay Cafe ☕
Society for Anime and Manga Appreciation (SAMA) and MCPC have come together to bring to you our long-awaited Cosplay Cafe!
Yes, you heard that right - the Cosplay Cafe is back again this year with our friends from SAMA to bring you some delicious food and drinks... whilst in Cosplay!
More details will be coming soon to our socials so keep posted 😊.
🎉 EXP 7 🎉
Come celebrate the end of exmas with our classic End of Exams Party!
We've once again collaborated with a huge range of your favourite special interest clubs to provide you the perfect send-off for your exams by mingling with friends, and relieving your post-exam stresses!
The clubs involved this year are:
Fantasy And Sci-Fi Association (FASA),
Monash Boardgames Society (MBS),
Monash Card Collective (MCC),
Monash Cosplay Club (MCPC) &
Monash University Role Player's Club (MURP).
EXP 7 will be held on Thursday the 24th of June so make sure to keep the date marked on your calendar!
More details will be coming soon to our Facebook page so keep an eye out for that 😊.
